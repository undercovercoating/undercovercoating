About us

Our Company

Undercovercoating LLC Is located in Cincinnati, Ohio and offers professional plasti dip spray booth services by appointment only.

Plasti Dip is a rubber coating that can be applied to just about anything. It's completely removable and it available in any color and finishes. We can take an old car and make it look cool again, or take a cool car and make it look even cooler. This helps to protect your car from everyday scratches and makes it look cool.

How It Work

Step 1

Once you have decided on placement of paint, your color is chosen send us an email to schedule an appointment.

Step 2

Come out to our shop. We can check out your ride and have a meeting about the process. After our meeting a deposit will be required to cover the cost of the product. This deposit can be made via paypal, cash or credit card.

Step 3

After confirmation of deposit an invoice will be sent to you. Once the plasti dip product is received we will send you an email to schedule installation date and time.
